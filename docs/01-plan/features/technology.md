# Plan: Technology & Certification

## Overview
Showcase the core technologies (BADP, MIAO) and certifications that validate the sustainability and performance of EcoBio products.

## Goals
- Explain the principle of biodegradation (BADP).
- Introduce freshness-keeping technology (MIAO/Quantum Mechanics).
- Present international and domestic certifications.
- Compare Ecofade technology with competitors (PLA, Oxo-biodegradable).
- Verify safety and environmental impact.

## Core Features
1. **Hero Section**: High-impact introduction to technology and certifications.
2. **Biodegradation Principles**: 4-step explanation of how the plastic breaks down.
3. **Freshness Technology**: Details on quantum mechanics, breathable film, and ethylene control.
4. **Certification Showcase**: List of key certifications with details.
5. **Comparison Table**: Side-by-side comparison with other biodegradable technologies.
6. **Safety & Environment**: Documentation of human and environmental safety.

## Technical Requirements
- Next.js (App Router)
- Tailwind CSS
- Responsive design
- Animation/Transitions for readability
